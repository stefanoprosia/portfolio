Quote Book

This is a quote book I designed using Adobe InDesign. For the theme of my quote book I chose to use sad lyrics from some of my favourite songs. All of the lyrics I chose are how I felt at the time. This project is very personal to me and even though I'm not in that place anymore, this book is still apart of me and how I felt. Each lyric uses the same font, album colours and themes that the original album artwork was designed.
