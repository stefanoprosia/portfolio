Planet Hair Logo Design

This is a logo design I created for my grandfathers barber shop. Before my design, he simply had a wordmark above his shop. I took the elements in the shops name "Planet Hair" and I created a modern logo that he could use for his store front and any business related promtional tools he may use to promote his business. I also created a full branding guide for him.
