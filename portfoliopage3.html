Nintendo Switch Brand Identity

I created a brand identity for Nintendo Switch using After Effects. I took clips of videos posted on YouTube of gameplay and trailers for the Nintendo Switch and created my own little brand identity video. I have audio that relates to the overall theme of the brand, I also have typography and colours that were used for the brand in the video. Overall the video looks like a commerical that can be used to promote the console and grab peoples attention.
